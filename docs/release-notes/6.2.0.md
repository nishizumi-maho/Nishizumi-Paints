# Nishizumi Paints 6.2.0

## Highlights

- Added **Showroom > Teams** for manual Trading Paints team paint downloads.
- Enter a Team ID plus a car ID, dashboard URL, car name, or iRacing car directory.
- The app tries session-aware team manifest payloads and saves matching team car, helmet, and suit assets when Trading Paints returns them.

## Output

- Default destination: `Documents\iRacing\paint`
- Team car paint: `paint\{car_directory}\car_team_{team_id}.tga`
- Team helmet: `paint\helmet_team_{team_id}.tga`
- Team suit: `paint\suit_team_{team_id}.tga`
- A custom one-time destination can still be selected from the Showroom tools.

## Privacy

This release does not add Trading Paints login storage. The manual team downloader sends the entered Team ID, resolved car directory, optional member IDs, and car number to Trading Paints manifest endpoints. It does not store cookies, passwords, or tokens.
