# Nishizumi Paints 6.4.2

## Fixed

- Showroom car dropdowns now close normally after a car is selected.
- Clicking a car selector opens the full Trading Paints car list again, with the native dropdown scroll behavior available.
- Typing in a car selector filters the dropdown and shows short inline suggestions without overflowing into the surrounding UI.
- Member ID and Teams all-paints downloads now sit in their own **Download everything** area to make the one-click full download easier to find.
