# Nishizumi Paints 6.9 Experimental

Draft experimental release focused on improving random fallback control and recovery.

## Added

- Random paint favorites and blocklist support.
- Session buttons to mark the selected random fallback as `Favorite` or `Block`.
- Paint history snapshots before replacing an iRacing paint file.
- `Restore previous` in the Session tab for car, helmet, and suit.
- Auto repair for failed Trading Paints direct downloads: normal retry first, partial-file cleanup, then one online retry.

## Notes

- Favorites are preferred during future random fallback when they match the item type and car directory.
- Blocked sources are excluded from future local RandomPool and public showroom random choices.
- History is stored locally under the app data paint-history folder and is capped per target.
