# Nishizumi Paints 6.8 Experimental

Draft experimental build focused on Team Session reliability and Session-tab diagnostics.

## Highlights

- Team driver swaps now go through a short confirmation step before the app clears and reapplies team paints.
- Confirmed swaps schedule short post-swap refresh retries at 5, 12, and 20 seconds.
- The Session table is compact outside Team sessions and shows the full Team diagnostics only when team targets are detected.
- Hovering `Car`, `Suit`, or `Helmet` in the Session table shows why that item is using Team paint, driver paint, random fallback, or iRacing default.
- Old Team-driver preload cache files are cleaned automatically.
