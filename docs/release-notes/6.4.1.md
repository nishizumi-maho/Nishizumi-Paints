# Nishizumi Paints 6.4.1

## Changed

- Reworked Showroom car downloads so the car is selected from the Trading Paints car dropdown instead of requiring TP car ID and slug fields.
- Car selectors in Showroom Car, Member ID, and Teams now open their dropdown when focused or clicked.
- Car selectors now filter as the user types and show matching suggestions below the field.
- Random Showroom car downloads continue to use the enabled Showroom source pool, choosing among enabled sources randomly.

- Compiled `Nishizumi_Paintsv6_nobrowser.py`.
