# Nishizumi Paints 6.3.1 Draft Release Notes

## Highlights

- Made **Showroom > Teams** more interactive for manual Trading Paints team downloads.
- Added live validation and a ready preview for Team ID, car resolution, resolved directories, member IDs, and car number.
- Added quick actions to fill the Teams form from the selected Session driver, clear the form, open the iRacing paint folder, and open Trading Paints showroom.
- Includes the 6.3.0 Advanced mode showroom source selector for Trending, Newest, Most favorited, Most raced (ever), or a random mix of selected sources.

## Notes

- Team downloads still use the no-browser session-aware manifest flow introduced in 6.2.0.
- Download buttons remain disabled until the required Team ID and car can be resolved.
